How to Prepare Your Vehicle for Long-Distance Transit

Moving a vehicle across South Africa requires careful planning to ensure the asset arrives in the same condition it left. At LN Mabaso Logistics, we prioritize safety above all else.

1. Clear the Vehicle

Remove all personal items, loose objects, and aftermarket accessories. This not only protects your belongings but allows our inspectors to verify the vehicle's interior condition easily.

2. Fuel Management

We recommend leaving your fuel tank at approximately one-quarter capacity. This reduces the vehicle's weight during transport and ensures enough fuel for short-distance maneuvering without adding unnecessary load stress.

Transit Security Note:

Maintaining a minimal fuel footprint helps control fleet load distributions efficiently across highways while satisfying regulatory cross-border transit security thresholds.

3. Exterior Inspection

Clean your vehicle before collection. A clean car makes it significantly easier to document any pre-existing scratches or dents, ensuring transparency and accountability for our insurance processes.
