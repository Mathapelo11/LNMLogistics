# 4K Image Generation Prompts

The image generation tool was not available in this session, so these prompts are ready to use for project assets when generation is available. Save final exports in `images/` and update the matching page image references.

## Hero Vehicle Logistics

Use case: photorealistic-natural  
Asset type: website hero image  
Primary request: A 4K hyperrealistic black and gold themed logistics scene for LN Mabaso Logistics and Car Movers, showing a premium car carrier truck on a clean South African highway with luxury vehicles secured for transport.  
Style/medium: photorealistic commercial automotive photography  
Composition/framing: wide landscape, strong first-viewport hero composition, usable darker space on the left for white headline text  
Lighting/mood: golden hour lighting, professional, premium, confident  
Color palette: black, white, metallic gold accents, natural road tones  
Constraints: no readable text, no invented logos, no watermark, no damaged vehicles
