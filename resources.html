FAQs

Answers before you book a vehicle move.

Use these quick answers to prepare a clear quote request.

How do I request a quote?

Open the quote form, enter your contact details, pickup location, delivery location, vehicle details and preferred date. The form sends the request to WhatsApp.

What number receives the quote?

All website quote requests are prepared for the business WhatsApp number +27 66 224 6679.

Do you move non-running vehicles?

You can request it. Add the non-running condition and any loading notes so the team can quote the correct handling method.

Can dealerships request multiple vehicle moves?

Yes. Add the fleet or dealer transfer service option and include the number of vehicles in the notes.

What details make a quote faster?

Pickup address, delivery address, vehicle make and model, vehicle condition, preferred date and special access notes.

Quick tip

More detail usually means a better quote.

Include vehicle condition, access limitations, loading instructions and the urgency of the movement.
